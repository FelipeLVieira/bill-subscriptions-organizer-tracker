## App Store / Play Store Strings

### Title
Bill Tracker - Subscriptions

### Short Description
Track bills, managing subscriptions & get payment alerts offline.

### Full Description
Never miss a payment again with Bill Tracker. A privacy-focused, offline-first application designed to help you organize all your recurring subscriptions and bills in one place.

**Key Features:**
- **Track Everything**: Manage Netflix, Spotify, Rent, Utilities, and more.
- **Privacy First**: 100% Offline. Your financial data stays on your device. We do not track you.
- **Custom Alerts**: Set reminders for upcoming bills so you're never late.
- **Insightful Analytics**: See where your money goes with monthly spending charts.
- **Flexible Intervals**: Support for Monthly, Yearly, Weekly, and Daily billing cycles.
- **Dark Mode**: Beautiful dark interface for night time usage.

### Keywords
bill tracker, subscription manager, spending tracker, offline finance, budget, reminders, recurring payments
