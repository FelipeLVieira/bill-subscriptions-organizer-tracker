Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

BMI Calorie Tracker ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Data Storage

  • All your health data (BMI records, calorie logs, meal entries, weight history) is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not operate any servers or cloud services for data storage.
  • Your health and fitness data never leaves your device.
  • No account or sign-up is required to use the app.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, address).
  • We do not collect health or medical data on any server.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not access your contacts.
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data.

Health Data

The app stores health-related information that you enter, including:

  • Body weight and height measurements
  • BMI calculations and history
  • Calorie intake and daily nutritional goals
  • Meal logs and food entries

All of this data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you reminders about logging meals or tracking your BMI. Notifications are processed locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: The free version displays non-personalized advertisements. AdMob may collect device information for ad serving and fraud prevention. We have configured AdMob to serve only non-personalized ads, meaning no user tracking data is collected for ad targeting. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions (Premium). RevenueCat processes purchase transactions through Apple's App Store. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

A premium subscription is available to remove ads. All purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

BMI Calorie Tracker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by:

  • Deleting the app from your device, which removes all stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
