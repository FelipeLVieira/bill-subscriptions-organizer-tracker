## Description (4000 characters max)
Bills & Subs Tracker helps you understand exactly where your money goes each month. Add all your recurring payments in one place and never be surprised by a charge again.

— Dashboard —
View your total spending at a glance. See monthly, weekly, and yearly summaries with clear statistics that help you make informed financial decisions.

— Bill Management —
Track any recurring payment: streaming services, utilities, rent, insurance, memberships, and more. Organize by category to understand your spending patterns. Support for multiple currencies included.

— Smart Reminders —
Receive notifications before bills are due. Choose when and how often you want to be reminded, giving you time to review subscriptions or ensure sufficient funds.

— Calendar View —
See all upcoming payments on a calendar. Identify busy payment periods and plan your finances around your schedule.

— Payment History —
Mark bills as paid and maintain a complete record of your payments. Attach receipts or documents for future reference.

— Privacy by Design —
Your financial data remains private. All information is stored locally on your device. No account required, no cloud sync, no data collection. Your bills are your business.

— Thoughtful Design —
A clean, intuitive interface with light and dark themes. The app adapts to your system preferences and stays out of your way.

— Continuous Improvement —
We actively develop and refine this app based on user feedback. If you have suggestions or encounter any issues, please reach out. Your input shapes the future of Bills & Subs Tracker.
